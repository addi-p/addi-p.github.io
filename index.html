about.

Who am I

I am Adrian Pelcaru, a theoretical physics PhD student at the Technical University Dresden. I graduated from the Humboldt University Berlin, and come from a biology and theoretical biophysics background.

My research revolves around Causal Inference in linear, nonlinear and chaotic time series, where I aim to develop new methods. On the applied side of things, I am interested in spread of infectious diseases, human mobility and environmental drivers thereof.

I also enjoy visualizing my work, thus bringing people close to the core principles of the methods or findings.
